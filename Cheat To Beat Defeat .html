Arithmetic Reasoning (AR)

Key Tips: Focus on word problems; identify key numbers and what’s being asked.

  • Distance = Rate × Time
  • Total Cost = Price per Item × Number of Items
  • Percent Change = ((New Value - Old Value) / Old Value) × 100

Mathematics Knowledge (MK)

Geometry:

  • Area of a Rectangle = length × width
  • Area of a Triangle = 1/2 × base × height
  • Volume of a Rectangular Prism = length × width × height
  • Pythagorean Theorem: a² + b² = c²

Algebra:

  • Solve for x: ax + b = c → x = (c - b) / a
  • Slope of a Line: m = Δy / Δx

Conversions:

  • 1 foot = 12 inches
  • 1 yard = 3 feet
  • 1 mile = 5280 feet
  • 1 gallon = 4 quarts
  • 1 pound = 16 ounces

Word Knowledge (WK)

Study Tips: Learn synonyms and antonyms.

Common prefixes:

  • un- (not)
  • re- (again)
  • pre- (before)
  • anti- (against)

Common suffixes:

  • -ful (full of)
  • -less (without)
  • -able (can be done)

General Test-Taking Tips

  • Answer easy questions first, then go back to harder ones.
  • Use the process of elimination.
  • Manage your time—don’t spend too long on one question.
